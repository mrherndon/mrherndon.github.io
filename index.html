Course Description

Algebra I students will study relationships between quantities and reasoning with expressions and equations, linear, exponential and quadratic functions and descriptive statistics. Problem solving, critical-thinking and making real world connections will be the focus of each student.

Classroom Goals and Objectives

Algebra 1 is a 1 credit, full year course. In this class students will learn relevant material and gain a deeper understanding of mathematical concepts. Students will develop a sense of creativity, learn to think intellectually, and to build confidence in problem solving. This year we will explore mathematical concepts that relate to linear and exponential equations, systems of linear equations and inequalities, quadratic equations, etc.

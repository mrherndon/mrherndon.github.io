Discipline Policies

Classroom Rules

  1. You are expected to maintain a respectful attitude to your peers, teachers, and their property.
  2. You are expected to come to class prepared, on time, and on task.
  3. Be aware that you are responsible for your own education.
  4. Personal Devices – including cell phones, smartwatches, earbuds/air pods, tablets and Chromebooks are to be used ONLY with teacher permission.
  5. Snacks/Drinks – Snacks are not allowed after the bell rings. Drinks are allowed if kept in the original container and have a lid. If a drink spills, it is the student’s responsibility to clean up. This is a peanut-free classroom.

Consequences

If these rules are broken, certain consequences will occur:

1st offense: documented warning
2nd offense: contact with parent/guardian and detention
3rd offense: referred to the Dean of Students.
Severe disruptions will be immediately referred to the office.

Attendance

Students are expected to attend class and to participate in the activities designated by the teacher each day. Attendance is the ultimate key to success in this class and absences, though sometimes unavoidable, should be minimized as much as possible. When absent, students will be required to seek missed instruction outside of the scheduled class time. It is also the student’s responsibility to check for missed work when absent. Please make an effort to schedule all appointments at other times during the day.
